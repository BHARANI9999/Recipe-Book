Glutten Balls

Discription

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 10| Cook Time : 25 | Total Time : 35

Ingredients

1.1 pound ground beef


2.1/2 to 2/3 cup crushed rice crackers


3.1/2 tablespoon dried oregano


4.1 teaspoon dried basil


5.1/2 teaspoon salt


Directions

1. Prepare a rectangular pan with tinfoil and a metal rack


2. Place all ingredients into a bowl and mix together by hand


3. Form into balls the size of ping pong or golf balls and place onto the rack in the pan


4. Cover the pan with tinfoil and place in the oven at 375 degrees for 25 minutes or until cooked through
