Vermicilli Kunafa (Arabic Dessert)

Discription

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 5| Cook Time : 25 | Total Time : 30

Ingredients

1.grams Vermicelli 125 gms


2.Mozzarella cheese 150 gms


3.butter 50 gms


4.sugar 1 cups


5.cardamom powder 1 tbsp


6.almond and Pista for garnishing 8-10 nos


7.orange colour 2 drop


8.butter for baking dish 1 tbsp


Directions

1. break Vermicelli into small pieces and mix it with butter nicely


2. add butter and two drops of orange colour in a baking dish and spread nicely.


3.Now add buttered vermicelli in it and press it nicely.


4. now add grated Mozzarella cheese on it and press from top and bake in preheated oven at 200 degree Celsius for 25 minutes.


5. for making sugar syrup add cardamom powder in warm water for 5 minutes.


6.Strain the water and add 1 cup sugar and lemon juice and cook for 3 to 4 minutes..


7.the sugar syrup is ready keep it aside.


8.when the Kunafa is nicely baked, let it sit for 5 minutes and then invert it on the serving dish.


9.Garnish it with almond and pista.
