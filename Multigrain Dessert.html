Multigrain Dessert

Discription

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 2| Cook Time : 10 | Total Time : 12

Ingredients

1.Ragi flour 100 gms


2.regular or Multigrain Oats 1/4 cups


3.wheat daliya 1/4 cups


4.Stevia/ Coconut sugar/Low calorie sugar or as per taste 1 tsp


5.salt 1/8 tsp


6.chopped almonds (optional) 4 tbsp


7.canola or olive oil 1 tsp


8.cardamom 1/4 tsp


9.water 1.5 cups


Directions

1. Mix ragi flour and salt. Steam for 3-4 minutes


2. Heat oil, add daliya and oats. Saute until golden brown. Add 1/2 cup water. Do not cover. Allow to thicken. Add Ragi flour, stevia and cardamom with 1/2 cup water. Mix well. Cover and cook for 2 minutes on low to medium flame


3. Now add another 1/2 cup water. Cover and cook for few minutes. If its too dry . Add little water.


4. Garnish with nuts. This is optional. Serve warm
