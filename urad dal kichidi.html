Urad Dal Kichidi

Discription

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 30| Cook Time : 20 | Total Time : 50

Ingredients

1.urad dal chilka 1 cups


2.basmati rice 2 cups


3.cumin 1 tsp


4.asefoetida 1 tsp


5.ghee 4 tbsp


6.sattu 1 cups


7.ginger garlic grated 1 tbsp


8.chopped onion 1 tbsp


9.achar ka masala 1 tbsp


10.chopped green chilli 1 tbsp


11.mustard oil 2 tbsp


Directions

1. Wash rice and dal soak fof 10 minutes


2. Take cooker add ghee, crackle heeng and cumin


3. Add soaked dal and rice


4. Adjust water


5. Add salt


6.Cook for 2 whistle.


7.For chokha


8.Add sattu garlic ginger onion achar ka mssala ,sakt grren chilli , mustard oil


9.Mix well


10.Add chopped corriander


11.Serve with khichri and dahi
