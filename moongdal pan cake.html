moongdal pan cake

Discription

yummy and healthy paranthas for kids tiffin

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 120| Cook Time : 10 | Total Time : 130

Ingredients

1.Moong Daal 1 cups


2.Dates,deseeded 3-4 nos


3.Cinnamon Powder 1/2 tsp


4.Honey 1 tbsp


5.Vanilla Essence 1/2 tsp


6.Water 1/4 cups


7.Oil 2 tbsp


Directions

1. Soak moongdal in water for 2 hours.


2. After 2 hours , grind it into a smooth paste along with dates.


3. Pour it into a bowl, add cinnamon powder, honey, vanilla essence and little water.


4. Mix well.Heat a non stick tawa, grease it with oil.


5.With a deep round spoon pour the batter from a little height into the pan.


6. Cook both the sides till it turns golden brown.


7.Top it with banana slices and honey before serving.
