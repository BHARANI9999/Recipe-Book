Ravva Upma

Discription

yummy and healthy paranthas for kids tiffin

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 4| Cook Time : 15 | Total Time : 19

Ingredients

1.Rava 1 cups


2.sliced onion 1 nos


3.chopped tomatoes 1 nos


4.grated carrot 1/4 cups


5.grated Ginger 1 tsp


6.mustard seeds 1 tsp


7.chana dal 1 tsp


8.urad dal 1 tsp


9.garam masala 1/2 tsp


10.salt to taste 1 tsp


11.water 2 1/2 cups


12,chopped green chillies 2 nos


13.dried Red Chillies 2 nos


14.coriander leaves 1 tbsp


15.cashew nuts 2 tbsp


Directions

1. dry roast Rava for 3 to 4 minutes and keep aside


2. don't over roasr the Rava don't let its colour change


3. heat oil in a pan


4.add mustard seeds, chana dal, urad dal and let them splutter


5. now add dried Red Chillies and Saute for a minute


6.add few curry leaves and saute for a minute


7.add grated ginger and green chillies and saute


8.now add sliced onion and saute till it is translucent


9.now add chopped tomatoes and grated carrot and saute for a minute


10.add cashew nuts and salt, mix nicely, cover and let it cool for 2 minutes.


11.open the lid and add fresh coriander leaves and mix.


12.now add water and cover the pan to give water a boil


13.when the water starts boiling simmer the heat and add the Rava and keep on stirring so that there are no lumps


14.Rava will Soak all The Water and will leave the sides of the pan


15.add juice of one lemon and mix nicely and let it cook for another 1 minute covered.


16.Rava Upma is ready garnish it with some coriander leaves and serve hot with Ginger chutney or coconut chutney
