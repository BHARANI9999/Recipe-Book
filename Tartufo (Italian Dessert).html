Tartufo (Italian Dessert)

Discription

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 240| Cook Time : 2 | Total Time : 242

Ingredients

1.3 small packs each of strawberry, mango and chocolate flavoured ice creams


2.1-2 tbsp. powdered oreo biscuit


3.1 tbsp. each of chopped pistachios, almonds and cherries to garnish


Directions

1. Fill a silicone mould / any disposable bowl / cup with mango flavoured ice cream. Make a hole in the centre with a spoon and fill some chocolate ice cream in it.


2. Again make a hole in the centre and fill it up with some strawberry ice cream. Cover the bowl with mango ice cream and freeze to set.


3. While serving, demould the tartufo and sprinkle the powdered oreo biscuit over it. Serve, garnished with chopped pista, almonds and cherry.
