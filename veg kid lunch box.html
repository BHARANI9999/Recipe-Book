Veg kids lunch box

Discription

A lunch box filled with scrumptious dishes for kids to enjoy their lunch time. Kids love to be enterained with varieties and I have compiled into delectable dishes for the little ones to munch on.

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 30| Cook Time : 30 | Total Time : 1H

Ingredients

1.For Masala Veg Idly : Idly batter 1 cup

2.1/3 cup finely chopped carrot, peas and corn

3.1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder

4.1/4 teaspoon chilli powder

5.1 teaspoon Ghee

6.2 Pinches of Himalaya salt for Idly batter

7.For Babycorn Capsicum Stir Fry : 3/4 cup chopped Baby corns

8.1/3 cup sliced Red and Yellow capsicum

9.1 Finely chopped onion

10.1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

11.1/2 teaspoon Chilli powder

12.1/4 teaspoon Himalaya salt

13.For Curd Rice : 1 Cup cooked rice

14.2/3 cup thick yogurt

15.1/4 teaspoon plus 2 pinches of Himalaya salt

16.1 teaspoon Soyabean flour

17.1" peeled Ginger

18.1/2 Green chilli

19.3 curry leaves

20.1 teaspoon chopped green coriander leaves

21.1 teaspoon Oil

22.1/4 teaspoon mustard seeds

23.1/4 teaspoon Urad dhal

24.1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida

25.For Dates Cashewnut Kozhukattai : 1/2 Cup rice flour

26.1 cup water

27.2 pinches of Himalaya salt

28.6 Pitted Dates

29.3/4 teaspoon Ghee

30.6 Cashew nuts

31.1 Apple

Directions

01 In a bowl add idly batter, soyabean flour, turmeric powder, chilli powder, vegetables, ghee and salt and mix together. Keep aside

02 For outer layer of Kozhukattai, boil 1 cup water, add 1/2 teaspoon ghee and salt. Mix rice flour slowly. Switch off the flame and close the lid for 3 minutes.

03 Grind green chilli, ginger, coriander leaves and curry leaves listed in Curd rice. Keep aside

04 Stuff dates with cashew nuts.

05 Babycorn capsicum stir fry : In a pan, heat oil and add onions, saute till it turns translucent.

06 Then add baby corns, turmeric powder, chilli powder and salt. Mix and cook for 2 minutes without adding water on medium flame.

07 Then add capsicum and cook for another 2 minutes. Remove from heat

08 By now the rice flour should be warm. Grease the kozhukattai/modhak mould with the remaining ghee listed in kozhukattai ingredients.

09 Fill the mould with rice flour and place the stuffed dates inside and close the bottom.

10 In the bottom idly plates grease and pour the vegetable idly batter.

11 On the top plate place the kozhukattai and steam for 7 minutes.

12 In a tempering pan heat oil, add mustard seeds, after it splutters add urad dhal and asafoetida.

13 Now add the ground ingredients ( green chilli, ginger, coriander leaves and curry leaves) and saute for 1.5 minutes.

14 In a bowl add rice and tempered ingredients.

15 Next add yogurt, salt and mix.

16 Wash the apple and drop in a hot water to get rid of covering wax. Cut into pieces and drop in water and wipe with a cloth.

17 Now pack the cooked dishes into a stainless lunch box.
