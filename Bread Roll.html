Bread Roll

Discription

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 10| Cook Time : 30 | Total Time : 40

Ingredients

1.All purpose flour (maida) 2 cups


2.Yeast 2 tsp


3.Sugar 1 tbsp


4.Salt 1 tsp


5.Butter 1 tsp


6.Paste:Milk (n Water if paste thickens) 1 cups


7.Cornflour 2 tbsp


8.Oil 3 tbsp


9.Chaat masala 1 dash


Directions

1. mix cornflour, chaat masala n milk well kip on flame n kip on stirring as it strts boiling add oil.


2. wen its thick liquid like remuv from flame.


3. mix dry ingredients very well add paste n bind to soft dough.


4. den kip in a big bowl n cover wid cling film.


5. let it fully rise punch bek n mould in small rolls or make big roll of any shape u desire n kip on greased tray, brush wid beaten egg o oil


6. let it fully rise n bake in oven on 200Â°C til top browned up cn tek 20-30mins.
