Nawabi Biriyani

Discription

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 10| Cook Time : 30 | Total Time : 40

Ingredients

1.basmati rice 1 1/2 cups


2.large potato 1 nos


3.peas(I used frozen ) 3 tbsp


4.onion sliced 1 nos


5.cumin seeds 1 tsp


6.water 500 ml


7.ghee 3 tbsp


8.For grinding : mint leaves 1/2 cups


9.chillies 2 nos


10.ginger garlic paste 2 tsp


11.garam masala 1/2 tsp


12.coconut milk 1/4 cups


13.salt as needed 1 pinch


Directions

1. Grind the ingredients given under "for grinding " & set aside.


2. Heat ghee in a pan.


3. Splutter cumin seeds.


4. Add onions and potatoes.


5. Add the ground masala.


6.Add rice and saute for a couple of minutes.


7.Add boiling water.


8.Cook until 3/4 th done.


9.Add green peas.


10.Cook further until completely done.


11.Serve with your favourite accompaniments.
