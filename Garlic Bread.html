Garlic Bread

Discription

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 120| Cook Time : 25 | Total Time : 145

Ingredients

1.All Purpose Flour: 2 cups (250 grams)


2.Dry Active Yeast: 1 Teaspoon


3.Salt: 3/4 Tmieaspoon


4.Sugar: 2 Tablespoons


5.Garlic powder: 2 Tablespoons


6.Milk: 1/2 Cup (125 ml) warm


7.Egg (large): 1 (Whisked)+1 for Egg wash


8.Unsalted butter: 3 Tablespoons (Softened)


Directions

1. All you need to do is keep adding the ingredients one by one in a large mixing bowl in the following order: Yeast, Sugar, Milk, Egg, Flour, Butter, Salt, Garlic powder. Keep stirring with the help of a strong wooden spatula.


2. The dough will be very sticky at this stage. Keep stirring until the dough comes together & it does not stick to the bowl any more. Should take about 15 minutes. *A stand mixer with the Dough Hook can be used.


3. Transfer the dough to a clean bowl. Cover tightly with cling wrap.


4. Let it rest for about 90 minutes. (1st Proofing).


5. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface.


6.Shape into a ball. Flatten it.


7.Cut into eight equal portions with the help of a serrated knife Or a sharp knife. Roll out into long strips. Roll the strips into oval buns.


8.Arrange in a baking dish lined with greased parchment paper.


9.Cover & let it rest for another 30 minutes. (2nd Proofing).


10.Preheat oven for 10 minutes at 180 Degree C/350 Degree F.


11.Brush generously with egg wash (Beaten egg). .


12.Bake for 25 minutes.


13.Note: 1. I have used American Cups Measurements.


14.2. Please use either a 13 X 9 Inches Rectangular Baking Dish Or a 10.5 Inches Round Baking Dish Or a Cast Iron Pan as I have used here.
