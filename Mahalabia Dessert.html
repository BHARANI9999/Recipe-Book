Mahalabia Dessert

Discription

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 60| Cook Time : 120 | Total Time : 120

Ingredients

1.milk 1 cups


2.cornflower 2 cups


3.red colour 1 tsp


4.full fat cream 2 cups


5.sugar 2 cups


6.water 1 cups


Directions

1. add milk on a pan.mix sugar,cornflower on it.mix it properly


2. now switch on the flame. on a low flame start mixing it


3. mix it continuesly


4. now after 5minutes it will start binding.now add cream.and mix immediately


5. mix well.


6. after 10minutes transfer it on a bowl.keep it on a fridge for atleast 2hours.


7.now for on a pan add 1cup water.boil it


8.add red food colour and sugar.mix it well.


9.now take 1tsp cornflower and mix it with cold water.and add it on boil red water


10.mix it for 10minutes.keep it completely cool.


11.now after 2hours pour red colour syrup on the bowl.


12.garnish with and dry fruits.


13.serve it cool.
