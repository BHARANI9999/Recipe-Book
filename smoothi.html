Smoothie

Discription

yummy and healthy paranthas for kids tiffin

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 2| Cook Time : 1 | Total Time : 3

Ingredients

1.Yellow banana: 1 nos


2.Oats: 2 tbsp


3.Almond milk / Diary milk: 1/2 cups


4.Chickoo: 1 nos


5.Blueberry jam: 1 tsp


6.Beetroot powder: 1 tsp


7.Flax seed powder: 1 tsp


8.Chia seeds: 1 tsp


Directions

1. Blend together all the ingredients except chia seeds into fine consistency.


2. Top it with chia seeds and serve.
