Cutli Aaloo With Pathiri Roti

Discription

yummy and healthy paranthas for kids tiffin

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 10| Cook Time : 10 | Total Time : 20

Ingredients

1.potato 3 us


2.oil 1 tbsp


3.jeera 1 tsp


4.mustard seeds 1 tsp


5.chilli flakes 1 tsp


6.pepper powder 1 tsp


7.seasme seeds 1 tsp


8.chaat masala 1 tsp


9.salt 1 tsp


10.turmeric powder 1/2 tsp


11.mint leaves 8 nos


12.Oil for fry potato rounds 3 tbsp


13.rice flour for pathiri roti 1 cups


14.water 1 cups


15.For roti salt 1 tsp


16.For roti oil 1 tsp


Directions

1. Cut potatoes in slices


2. And fry them


3. Heat oil add jeera and rai


4. Add seasme seefs,haldi and salt on low flame


5. oast on tawa adding little ghee on both sides , till cooked and golden Brown in colour


6. Add ketch up and chopped mint leaves.


7. Add potato slices and mix all of them


8. Sprinkle flakes and coriander leaves


9. For pathiri roti heat water in a pan


10. Add salt and oil


11. When water boils add rice flour


12.Mix properly and cover for 5 min.


13.Transfer in a plate


14.Bring together with hands and make dough


15.Grease with oil and keep covered


16.Take a ball sprinkle dry rice flour and roll down


17.Cut round with a lid


18.Then flip it and cook from other side


19.Our soft pathiri rotis are ready
