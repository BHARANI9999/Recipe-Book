Layered Dessert

Discription

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 20| Cook Time : 25 | Total Time : 45

Ingredients

1.For first layer-3/4 ltr milk


2.1 cup cooked rice


3.1 tsp cardamom powder


4.1/4 cup chopped dry fruits


5.4 tbsp sugar or per your taste


6.For 2nd layer -1/2 ltr milk


7.2-3 tbsp vanilla custard powder


8.1 tsp vanilla essence


9.2-3tbsp sugar or per your taste


10.3/4 cup chopped mix fruits or as required


11.For 3rd layer -1 cup chocolate ice cream and pomegranate seeds for garnishing


Directions

1. For 1st layer - Boil the milk in a heavy bottom pan,after a boil add boiled rice and cook till it gets thicken. Now add sugar cardamom powder and chopped dry fruits. mix well and cook for 3-4 minutes. Let it cool.


2. for 2nd layer - In another pan boil milk with sugar.after a boil add vanilla custard powder (after making a paste with some milk) and vanilla essence,cook it on low flame and keep stirring continuously to avoid any lumps, When it gets thicken switch off the flame..Let it cool. Then add chopped fruits and keep it in the fridge.


3. Now arrange the layers in a serving bowl.For first' layer add rice kheer.for 2nd layer add cool fruit custard and the third layer add chocolate ice cream.


4. Now garnish with grated chocolate and fruits. Serve chilled.
