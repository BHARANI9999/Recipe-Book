Paneer Carrot Paranthas

Discription

yummy and healthy paranthas for kids tiffin

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 15| Cook Time : 20 | Total Time : 35

Ingredients

1.For the dough :wheat flour mein 2 cups


2.salt 1/4 tsp


3.red chilly powder 1/4 tsp


4.milk 2 tbsp


5.ghee for roasting 1 tbsp


6.For the filling :homemade paneer 1 cups


7.carrots grated, 2 nos


8.chopped coriander leaves, 1/4 teaspoon red chilly powder ( optional ) 1 tbsp


9.garam masala 1/2 tsp


Directions

1. Mix all milk and make a smooth pliable dough.


2. Cover with a lid and keep aside for 5 minutes.


3. In another bowl mix all the ingredients for the filling


4. Now take a portion of dough and fill the Paneer mixture ,make a ball , now roll into a parantha of medium thickness


5. oast on tawa adding little ghee on both sides , till cooked and golden Brown in colour


6. Serve with ketchup or mayonnaise..
