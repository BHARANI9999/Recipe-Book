Save Tomato

Discription

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 5| Cook Time : 15 | Total Time : 20

Ingredients

1.Tomatoes 5 mediums


2.Besan sev 3/4 cup


3.Water 1cup


4.Turmeric pwd 1/2 tsp


5.Red chilli pwd 1 1/4 tsp


6.Dhaniya jeera ( corriander cumin )pwd 1 tsp.


7.Salt 1 tsp.


8.Sugar 2 tsp.


9.Garlic paste 1 tsp.


10.Oil 2 1/2 tbsp.


11.Mustard seeds 1/2 tsp.


12.Cumin seeds 1/2 tsp.


13.Hing 1/4 tsp.


14.Corriander leaves chopped 2 tbsp.


Directions

1. Cut tomatoes in to small pieces


2. Heat oil in a pan


3. Add mustard seeds n cumin


4. As this splutters add hing n garlic paste n saute for 1/2 minute n add tomatoes n stir


5. Cover n cook for 1 minute


6.Stir n add salt 1/2 cup water mix n cover


7.Cook for 5 minutes


8.Add remaining water red chilli pwd dhaniya jeera pwd sugar cover n let it cook for 1 minute n switch off the gas


9.Add sev mix n cover for 5 minutes


10.Serve in plate n garnish with corriander
