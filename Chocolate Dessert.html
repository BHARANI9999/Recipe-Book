Chocolate Dessert

Discription

Cooking Time

Preparation Time : 30| Cook Time : 10 | Total Time : 40

Ingredients

1.1 cup bread crumbs


2.s7 parle G biscuit


3.2 tablespoon chocolate syrup


4.2 tablespoon whipped cream


5.5 oreo biscuit


6.1 dairy milk chocolate


7.1 teaspoon sugar


8.1/2 cup milk


9.2 Chocolate stick


Directions

1. Make a powder of parle g biscuit


2. Crushed Oreo biscuit in pieces


3. In a bowl add bread crumbs parle g biscuit powder sugar and mix well


4. Now add milk in it and make a moist mixture


5. Take a serving bowl first put biscuit and bread crumbs mixture


6.Now spread some chocolate syrup


7.Now put crushed oreo


8.Again put bread crumbs and biscuit mixture now put some chocolate syrup


9.Now spread wipe cream put some chocolate syrup and crushed chocolate


10.Served chilled
